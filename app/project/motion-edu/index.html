Motion EDU

Designing personalized e-learning experiences for students

An extended arm of school curriculum

Being a leading education service provider, Motion Education’s learning app ‘VIDYATRI’ aimed at helping the school students prepare for India’s competetive exams effectively.

  • Client

    Motion Education

  • Industry

    EdTech

  • Type

    Web, Ipad, Mobile App

UX Strategy

  • Aligning with Stakeholders

    Stakeholders Workshop

    By directly engaging with key players, we were able to get crucial knowledge, fill gaps, identify potential users, context and align with their goals.

  • Understanding Students’ Behaviour

    User Research

    Analyzing students’ learning pattern & behaviour through research helped us to arrive at their major painpoints in preparing for exams.

  • Designing their experience

    Defining , Ideating & Modelling

    After problem definition, we mapped their journey of exam-taking and modelled features that would help them learn & prepare better.

  • Creating the visual experience

    UI Design

    Fun & gamified elements of colour, type & patterns were designed to motivate and make the preparation an exciting & rewarding journey.

Staying ahead with insights

The main dashboard enables the student to view his progress across subjects with an overview of recent tests, video lessons and productivity insights.

Enabling helpful practice sessions

With a lot of importance given to taking mock tests, we empowered students with easy practice tests with accurate and immediate results across web and mobile.
